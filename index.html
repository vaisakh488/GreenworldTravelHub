About Us

Welcome to Green World Travel Hub, a trusted travel and tour operator proudly based in the heart of Kerala. We specialize in crafting safe, comfortable, and memorable travel experiences across Kerala, South India, and select destinations beyond.

With years of hands-on experience in the tourism and transportation industry, we have built a strong reputation for reliability, personalized service, and well-planned itineraries. Our team is dedicated to showcasing the breathtaking natural beauty, rich culture, and heritage of Kerala while ensuring a smooth and enjoyable journey for every traveler.

One of our key strengths is our extensive fleet of vehicles, which includes luxury buses, mini buses, tempo travellers, SUVs, and other well-maintained vehicles. This allows us to offer flexible vehicle selection based on group size, comfort preferences, and travel requirements, making every trip efficient and hassle-free.

To enhance the Kerala experience, we also provide houseboat services, giving travelers the opportunity to explore the serene backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom while enjoying traditional hospitality, scenic views, and peaceful surroundings.

Our services cover a wide range of travel needs, including educational tours for schools and colleges, devotional and pilgrimage trips, customized family and group tours, special event trips, and reliable vehicle rental services for short and long journeys.

Whether you are planning a school excursion, a spiritual retreat, a honeymoon, a corporate outing, or a family vacation, Green World Travel Hub is committed to delivering safe travel, transparent pricing, and unforgettable experiences.
